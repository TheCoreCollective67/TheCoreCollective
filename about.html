Welcome!

Hello! I’m Ella, the founder of The Core Collective. I created this site to help teens discover the fun, strength-building world of Pilates. Whether you’re using a reformer or just a mat, my goal is to provide safe, engaging exercises for every skill level.

My Journey

I’ve been practicing Pilates with my mentor Michelle and love sharing it with teens to improve core strength, flexibility, and confidence. This site combines detailed instructions with photos to make learning easy and enjoyable.
